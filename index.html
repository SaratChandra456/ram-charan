Devotional Places in Srikakulam

Sri Kshatriya Simha Rameshwara Temple

The Sri Kshatriya Simha Rameshwara Temple, located in the Srikakulam district, is a significant pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is known for its ancient architecture and serene environment. Devotees visit the temple to seek blessings and for the spiritual tranquility it offers.

Sri Suryanarayana Swamy Temple

Located in Arasavilli, the Sri Suryanarayana Swamy Temple is dedicated to the Sun God, Surya. It is one of the few temples in India dedicated exclusively to Surya. The temple is known for its architectural beauty and is a popular destination for devotees seeking blessings for good health and prosperity.
